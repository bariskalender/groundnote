# GroundNote Demo Questions

Use these questions with `groundnote-demo-handbook.md`. The final question is deliberately
unsupported and should produce an evidence-based refusal without fabricated citations.

## Easy

1. How often does each Lantern Node record a sample?
2. Which node is deployed in Willow?
3. How long are raw CSV files retained after the class presentation?
4. What battery voltage is required during the morning check?
5. Which room had the fewest missing samples?

## Medium

1. Compare the pilot observations for Maple and Willow.
2. Why does the team calculate five-minute medians while retaining the raw samples?
3. What steps are taken when a node is moved, covered, or exposed to water?
4. How do the analyst and reviewer roles reduce the risk of unsupported claims?
5. Explain the difference between the incident procedure and the normal sensor-fault procedure.

## Summary

Summarize the deployment plan, data-quality rules, and main pilot limitations in a short study note.

## Deliberately Unanswerable

What orbit does the Lantern Lab weather satellite use, and when was it launched?
